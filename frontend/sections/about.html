About Sri Laxmi
Industries

Sri Laxmi Industries is a precision engineering and manufacturing company based in India with over three decades of experience producing high-quality mechanical components for industrial clients.

Our facility is equipped with modern CNC machining centres, conventional lathes, drilling machines, and metal cutting equipment — allowing us to handle both small batch custom work and larger production runs.

We supply components directly to engineering companies, OEM manufacturers, and procurement teams across India, and are actively expanding our reach to clients in Europe, Ireland, and North America.
